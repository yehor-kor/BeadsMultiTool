About me

My name is Tatiana Korablina. I am the owner of an online beaded jewelry store KorTaniaHandMadeShop and an online shop of beaded patterns for my author's jewelry KowShop on the ETSY platform.

I'm from Ukraine. I have been doing beaded needlework for over 10 years. I work with beads in different techniques. I also weave a lot of beaded earrings.

To design my designer earrings, I created a small but very convenient computer program together with my programmer son. It is very convenient to draw patterns of earrings in different techniques: single brick stitch, double brick stitch and triple brick stitch.

In this program, unfortunately, there is no choice of color indicating the numbers and names of the beads, but here it is very convenient to simply color the earring patterns. The pattern you created can be saved on your computer and edited at any time if necessary.

I've been using this program for a long time and I'm very happy with it! I wish you the same!) It is much more convenient than drawing on printed paper templates. After all, here you can endlessly correct and change the pattern and colors and save it as a file.

Have fun drawing and creating unique beaded earrings!!!)))
